About Me

Bruno de Athayde Prata is a professor in the Department of Industrial Engineering at the Federal University of Ceará (UFC), Brazil. He holds a Ph.D. in Industrial Engineering and Management from the Faculty of Engineering of the University of Porto, Portugal (2011). His main research interests include production scheduling, optimization, computational intelligence, and logistics. He's also a researcher on productivity at CNPq - National Council for Scientific and Technological Development, since 2019. He serves on the editorial board of Engineering Optimization (Associate Editor in Industrial Engineering and Operations Management), Journal of Project Management, and Jurnal Optimasi Sistem Industri.
